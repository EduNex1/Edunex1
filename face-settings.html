How It Works
1 When a face is recognized before the late deadline, attendance is marked as Present (P).
2 When a face is recognized after the late deadline, attendance is marked as Late (L).
3 After the Absent Cutoff Time, all students/staff who haven't been recognized are automatically marked as Absent (A).
4 If someone marked Absent later shows their face, their status automatically changes to Late (L).
5 Each person can only be marked once per day — duplicate recognition is ignored.
6 On Sundays, Holidays (from holiday list), and 2nd Saturdays (if enabled) — no auto-absent marking happens. Applies to both students & staff.
